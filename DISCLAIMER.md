# Disclaimer

BabySafety is an informational tool only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult your healthcare provider (OB-GYN, pediatrician, or other qualified medical professional) before making decisions about product use during pregnancy, nursing, or for your baby.

The safety ratings and information provided by this tool are based on publicly available research and guidelines from organizations such as the FDA, ACOG, AAP, and EWG. However, individual circumstances vary, and new research may change current recommendations.

This tool does not account for individual medical conditions, allergies, drug interactions, or other personal health factors.
